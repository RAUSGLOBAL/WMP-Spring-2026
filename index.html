Session 1
Three Perspectives on Optimizing Content Budgets
1
Reframe how you evaluate bids Value delivered per dollar, not just the bottom line.
2
Production guidelines are your most underused lever Build them now. Procurement leads the process.
3
Agency markup transparency is a real negotiating point Moving from nice-to-have to standard. Use it.
4
State production tax rebates are real budget recovery Procurement is the right person to drive this.
5
Spend intelligence tools give procurement a real seat in pre-production Close the knowledge gap before the next planning cycle.
Session 2
GenAI 101: What to Know + How It Works
1
AI cost savings are real Only when deployed against the right brief by the right people.
2
Brand AI and automated AI are not the same service Buying the wrong one at the wrong price is already happening.
3
R&D is a legitimate pre-production phase It belongs in the SOW and the budget (typically 10-15% of project cost).
4
AI tool costs are a real and variable line item Don't let them disappear into vendor margins.
5
Highest-ROI AI use cases involve logistics complexity Procurement's cost history is the map.
Session 3
Leading a Creative Operations Transformation
1
Every org is pressured to "do something with AI" Procurement can be the one who shapes what that actually means.
2
Technology without adoption is abandonware Build the change management plan before you sign the license.
3
Your AI addendum conversation is the most valuable one you're not having Audit contracts. Develop a standard template with legal.
4
Start with the pain, not the possibility Use cases that solve real problems get adopted. Trend-chasing doesn't.
5
Vendor relationships built before AI are due for review Incumbency is no longer the same thing as capability.
Session 4
Before You Sign: A Legal Update on AI
1
One pre-bid question changes everything Is this a digital twin or a synthetic performer? Ask it first.
2
Brands cannot own AI-generated output Your current contracts probably assume they can. Audit now.
3
New York's AI disclosure law takes effect June 2026 If you're running AI-assisted advertising, act now.
4
Content provenance is becoming a compliance obligation Start keeping masters with their metadata now.
5
Small production partners may not protect you Your brand pays if they don't. Build tiered AI compliance requirements.
